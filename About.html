About Book Store

Book Store is one of India’s largest dedicated online bookseller, offering one of the largest range of titles in India along with stationary and office supplies at lower rates and feasible prices. Founded in 2014 to make ‘All Books available to the readers and customers’ we focus on selling also from the regional authors and in regional languages differentiating ourselves from other retailers who increasingly focus on bestsellers.


All books available to All

Currently, Book Store is able to ship millions of unique titles, within defined time period, with the figure increasing every day. Apart from main publishing houses we even list and supply books from other retailers and focus on regional books, authors & publishers.
